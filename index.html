The artist

Meet Lathika

“I don’t paint flowers — I grow them, petal by petal, out of clay.”

For years Lathika has shaped air-dry clay into roses, lotuses, lilies and leaves so lifelike they seem freshly cut — and her practice reaches beyond clay into figurative sculpture, vivid paintings and pencil sketches, all made entirely by hand.

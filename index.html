The artist

Meet Lathika

“I don’t paint flowers — I grow them, petal by petal, out of clay.”

For years Lathika has shaped air-dry clay into roses, lotuses, lilies and leaves so lifelike they seem freshly cut. Her work spans framed relief panels, sculpted vessels and figurative pieces — all made entirely by hand.
